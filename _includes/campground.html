Our Campground

We offer a full service campground. We serve Motor Homes as well as primitive campers.

Our full-hook up campground provides water, sewer, and electric at each site. Each site is set up with a fire pit and a picnic table. We have 31 full-hook up sites. We also have a primitive area. If you are interested in a camp site we recommend you call ahead and reserve your site. If you plan on cancelling to get a full refund you must call 7 days in advance, otherwise there is a $25.00 cancellation fee.

Full-hook up sites: $40.00 per night
Primitive sites: $20.00 per night.
