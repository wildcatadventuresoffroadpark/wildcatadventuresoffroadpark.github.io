Our Campground

We offer a full service campground. We serve Motor Homes as well as primitive campers.

Our full-hook up campground provides water, sewer, and electric at each site. Each site is set up with a fire pit and a picnic table. We have 42 full-hook up sites. We also have a primitive area. If you are interested in a camp site we recommend you call ahead and reserve your site.

Full-hook up sites: $43.00 per night
Primitive sites: $22.00 per night.

Primitive Camping spots are avaliable on a "First Come, First Serve" basis

Campground Cancellation Policy

If you have reserved a campground spot and need to cancel your reservation, there will be a $25.00 cancellation fee. If your cancellation is within 7 days of the reservation you will be charged 50% of the total reservation amount.
