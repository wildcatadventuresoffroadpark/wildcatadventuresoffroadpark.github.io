A little about us

New, family focused, 1,800 acre off-road park that will accommodate all off-road enthusiasts. In the near future there will be loads of activities and events for all ages to enjoy. There are several types of trails to accommodate different vehicles.

Park office is open 9:00 a.m. to 5:00 p.m. Monday through Friday and 8:00 a.m. to 5:00 p.m. on Saturday and Sunday. Trails are open 24 hours per day, seven days per week.

Adult riders are $12.00 per day, children 12 and under are $7.50 per day, and children 5 and under are free. Anyone under the age of 18 must be accompanied by an adult.
